About Kroptek

Kroptek is a UK Agritech company that provides LED grow lights and develops Controlled-Environment Agriculture (CEA) solutions. We thrive to be innovative, customer-focused and sustainability-driven, which is the foundation of our company's core values. Our objective is to provide high quality food at a consistent quantity and quality, anywhere in the world and anytime of the year at the lowest cost to consumers with a minimum impact on the environment. Based in the UK and being one of the few Agritech companies with its own LED factory, Kroptek has the ability to customise solutions and build cost-efficient and high-quality products quickly. Our global client base includes food and ornamentals growers, horticulture professionals, as well as research organisations and universities.
